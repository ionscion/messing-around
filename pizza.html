Homemade Italian-Style Pizza



What you'll need

  • *A quality baking/cooking scale is recommended*
  • Pizza stone for the oven
  • King Arthur '00 Pizza Flour - 2 cups
  • 1/8 tsp Active Dry Yeast
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 3/4 cup lukewarm water
  • Pizza sauce - Barilla makes a decent one
  • Fresh Mozzarella
  • Pepperoni or whatever other toppings you might want.
  • Fresh basil



Instructions

  1. Mix together the dry ingredients for the dough. At this point it's a good idea to whisk the flour to ensure there are no clumps.
  2. Form the dough into a ball.
  3. Place formed dough into a large Pyrex mixing bowl.
  4. Cover bowl and leave to rise at room temperature for 24hrs.
